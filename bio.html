Brief Bio

Dr. María Pereda is a complex systems researcher working as a lecturer at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. Her research focuses on the understanding of human behaviour by means of models and experiments, as well as on the application of Machine Learning techniques to uncover patterns from digital traces. Her interests are studying complex systems and emergent phenomena. The main methods in her research are simulation modelling, behavioural economics experimentation, machine learning, network theory, and game theory.
