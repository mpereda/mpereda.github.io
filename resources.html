Resources

CODE

clusterBip: a Python function to cluster bipartite data sets based on the statistical significance of coincidences. Available at https://github.com/mpereda/clusterBip. Paper preprint in ArXiv.

Code for the course Introduction to socio-economic experiments with oTree: https://github.com/mpereda/SocioComplexSS.

DATASETS

Large scale and information effects on cooperation in public good games. Available at Zenodo.

Group size effects and critical mass in public goods games. Available at Zenodo.

MODELS

Cooperation Under Resources Pressure (CURP): an agent-based model designed to explore the evolution of cooperation under different resources scenarios that condition individual needs for survival.

WWHW Wave When the Hale Wale 2.0 model: an agent-based model designed to explore the development of cooperation in hunter-fisher-gatherer societies that face a dilemma of sharing an unpredictable resource that is randomly distributed in space.

Quality uncertainty and market failure model: interactive simulation game specially designed to teach the market effects of quality uncertainty.
