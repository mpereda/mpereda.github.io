Welcome!

Welcome to my personal webpage where you will find information about what I am working on, my CV, and other professional stuff. I have always loved learning and understanding the beautiful and complex world we live in. I started my research adventure nine years ago, and I keep been amazed with the world out there.

My interests are very interdisciplinary, but focused on the study of complex systems and emergent phenomena. Specifically, I work on understanding human behaviour (mainly but not only, related to socio-economic dilemmas) by means of experimentation, modelling and data analysis.

My goal as a researcher is to make interesting and high quality contributions as well as to have fun with science.
